Protein-Packed Energy Bites

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 100g rolled oats
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter (or almond butter)
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (choose a natural one with no added sugars)
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional)
  • 2 tbsp dark raw cacao nibs (optional for extra flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Mash the banana: In a medium-sized bowl, mash the ripe banana with a fork until it becomes a smooth puree.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In another bowl, combine the rolled oats, protein powder, chia seeds, cinnamon, and a pinch of sea salt. Stir well to ensure the dry ingredients are evenly mixed.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients: Add the peanut butter, raw honey, and the mashed banana to the dry ingredients. Mix thoroughly until everything is well combined. You should have a thick, dough-like consistency.
  4. Add cacao nibs (optional): If you want to add some extra crunch and chocolate flavor, fold in the raw cacao nibs.
  5. Form the energy bites: Using your hands, roll the mixture into small bite-sized balls (about 1-inch diameter). You should be able to make 8–10 bites, depending on the size.
  6. Chill: Place the energy bites in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to firm up.
  7. Serve and enjoy: Once chilled, they’re ready to eat! Store the energy bites in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week.

Enjoy!

These bites are perfect as a post-workout snack, a mid-day energy boost, or a healthy treat when you're craving something sweet!
