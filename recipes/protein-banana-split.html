Protein Banana Split

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas>
  • 500g low-fat quark
  • 2 tbsp raw honey
  • 30g dark raw cacao chocolate (at least 85% cacao)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of sea salt (optional for enhanced flavor)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the bananas:
    Peel the bananas and slice them lengthwise down the middle. Arrange the two banana halves in a bowl or on a plate to form the base of your "split."
  2. Make the protein "ice cream":
    In a small bowl, mix the low-fat quark with 1 tablespoon of raw honey. If you want a creamier texture, you can gently whip the mixture with a fork or a hand mixer. Add the vanilla extract and a pinch of sea salt, if desired, to enhance the flavor. This will act as your "ice cream" substitute.
  3. Prepare the chocolate drizzle:
    Break the dark raw cacao chocolate into small pieces and melt it slowly in a heatproof bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler method) or in short bursts in the microwave. Once melted, stir in the remaining tablespoon of raw honey for sweetness.
  4. Assemble the Protein Banana Split:
    Spoon the quark mixture generously over the banana halves. Drizzle the melted chocolate over the top, ensuring each portion gets a nice amount of the dark chocolate goodness.
  5. Optional toppings:
    If you want to add a bit more texture or flavor, you can sprinkle crushed nuts, seeds, or a few extra cacao nibs on top.
  6. Serve and enjoy:
    Your protein-packed, wholesome banana split is ready to enjoy!
